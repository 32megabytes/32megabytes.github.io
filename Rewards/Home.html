Welcome to the Finding Subtext Rewards Center!

This is an exclusive place where Finding Subtext donors can access rewards regardless of the donation platform used. Think of this as the Patreon-exclusive feed but given to all donors irrespective of their platform of choice.

How do I access these perks?

Passwords are provided within applicable donor platforms. These codes will change at random intervals to prevent abuse. If you lose access but are still subscribed as a monthly donor, please check your chosen donor platform for an updated password.

What is being offered on here?

Donor perks can range depending on what's offered. Examples of rewards include: early-access content, "behind the scenes" content, updates on active projects, access to unreleased or scrapped videos, exclusive videos, and more. Visit FindingSubtext.com/donate for more information.
